Our Story

From a Single Van to a Global Network

Zenith was founded in 2010 by two logistics veterans who believed courier delivery could be smarter, more transparent, and more human. We started with one van in New York City and a single promise: your package, on time, every time.

Today, Zenith operates across 200+ countries, maintains 85+ distribution hubs, and has delivered over 12 million packages for businesses ranging from solo sellers to Fortune 500 enterprises. Technology drives every decision — from AI route optimisation to our real-time tracking platform.
